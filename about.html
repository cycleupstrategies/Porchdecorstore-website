About PorchDecor

Connecting communities through the celebration of seasons

Our Mission

PorchDecor exists to strengthen communities by connecting local growers, logistics partners, and homeowners around the simple joy of celebrating seasons. We believe that a beautifully decorated porch isn't just about aesthetics - it's about expressing pride in your home, supporting local agriculture, and marking the passage of time with intention and beauty.

Every pumpkin, wreath, and bouquet tells a story - of a farmer who planted seeds with care, a delivery partner who ensured safe arrival, and a homeowner who welcomes guests with seasonal warmth. We're building the platform that brings these stories together.

The Problem We're Solving

For years, we've watched the same pattern repeat:

  • Homeowners drive from farm to farm, garden center to garden center, searching for quality seasonal decor - wasting time and gas
  • Local growers struggle to reach customers directly, forced to sell through middlemen who take massive cuts
  • Seasonal decorations shipped from across the country or imported, when exceptional products are grown locally
  • Logistics complexity prevents small farms from offering delivery, limiting their market
  • Community connections between growers and consumers are lost in traditional retail

We knew there had to be a better way - a platform that would make locally-sourced seasonal decor as convenient as ordering anything else online, while ensuring growers receive fair compensation and customers enjoy exceptional quality.

Our Vision for the Future

We're starting with porch decor, but we see a much bigger opportunity:

  • Expanding Product Categories: From seasonal decor to potted plants, garden supplies, and fresh flowers
  • Year-Round Service: Making local agriculture accessible every season, not just fall and winter
  • National Network: Replicating our model in every region of America where seasons change
  • Education Platform: Teaching people about agriculture, seasonal cycles, and sustainable practices
  • Farm Tourism: Connecting customers directly with farms for tours and pick-your-own experiences
  • Technology Innovation: Using data and logistics optimization to make local agriculture more profitable

Ultimately, we envision a future where buying local is as easy as buying from anywhere, where small farms thrive by connecting directly with their communities, and where every neighborhood celebrates the seasons together.

Our Story

PorchDecor was born from a simple observation: people love decorating their porches for seasons and holidays, but the process of sourcing quality local products is unnecessarily difficult.

Our founder spent years watching families drive to multiple farms looking for the perfect pumpkins, only to settle for inferior imported products at big box stores. Meanwhile, exceptional local farms struggled to reach customers, limited by geography and logistics.

The solution seemed obvious - create a platform connecting these groups. But execution is everything. We spent months talking to growers about their needs, studying logistics models, understanding what customers truly value. We built relationships before we built technology.

Now we're ready to launch, starting in select regions and expanding based on what we learn. This is just the beginning of the PorchDecor story, and we'd love for you to be part of it.

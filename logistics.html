Your Typical Day

  • Check the app for available routes and claim your deliveries
  • Pick up pre-packaged orders from designated farms or hubs
  • Follow GPS-optimized routes with 5-15 stops per route
  • Deliver products to porches with care and professionalism
  • Capture delivery confirmation photos via the app
  • Report any issues immediately through our support system
  • Get paid within 48 hours of completed deliveries

Requirements

Vehicle Requirements

  • Pickup truck, cargo van, SUV, or larger vehicle
  • Able to transport 8-20 orders per route (varies by season)
  • Clean, well-maintained, and reliable
  • Bed cover or enclosed cargo area recommended for weather protection
  • Vehicle must be no more than 15 years old

Insurance & Legal

  • Valid driver's license and clean driving record
  • Commercial auto insurance or willingness to obtain it
  • General liability insurance (we can help you get affordable coverage)
  • Pass background check and MVR review
  • Business license (for business entities)

Technology Requirements

  • Smartphone (iOS or Android) for delivery app
  • GPS capability for navigation
  • Camera for delivery confirmation photos
  • Reliable internet/data connection

Physical Requirements

  • Ability to lift 50+ pounds repeatedly
  • Comfortable loading/unloading products carefully
  • Able to work outdoors in various weather conditions
  • Navigate stairs, walkways, and various terrain types

Compensation

How You'll Earn

Multiple earning opportunities:

  • Per-Delivery Rate: $8-15 per delivery based on package size and delivery complexity
  • Mileage: $0.67 per mile for distances beyond pickup location
  • Route Bonuses: Complete 10+ deliveries in one route for bonus payments
  • Peak Season Multipliers: 1.5x rates during highest demand periods
  • Perfect Delivery Bonuses: Weekly bonuses for 100% on-time, damage-free deliveries

Earnings Potential

  • Part-Time Contractors: $500-1,500 per week during peak seasons
  • Full-Time Partners: $2,000-4,000 per week during peak seasons
  • Small Business Partners: $10,000-30,000+ per month during peak seasons

Payment Terms

  • Paid via direct deposit within 48 hours of delivery completion
  • Weekly payment summaries and tax documents provided
  • Transparent tracking of all deliveries and earnings
  • No hidden fees or deductions

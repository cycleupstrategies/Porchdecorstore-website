Grow Your Business With PorchDecor

You've built your farm through hard work, dedication, and a commitment to quality. Now it's time to reach more customers who appreciate what you grow. PorchDecor connects your farm directly with homeowners seeking authentic, farm-fresh seasonal products.

  • Guaranteed Market: We connect you with customers before you plant, reducing risk and waste
  • Fair Pricing: You set your prices; we ensure you receive fair compensation for quality products
  • Year-Round Opportunities: Multiple seasons mean consistent income throughout the year
  • Logistics Support: We handle pickup and delivery - you focus on growing
  • Marketing Power: Your farm story reaches thousands of potential customers
  • Community Connection: Build relationships with customers who value local agriculture

Become a Porch Decor Store Entrepreneur

Beyond being a supplier, exceptional growers can become PorchDecor Store Entrepreneurs - operating as mini-franchises in their region.

What Is a Store Entrepreneur?

Store Entrepreneurs are growers who:

  • Serve as regional hubs for their area
  • Coordinate with other local growers to fulfill orders
  • Earn additional income through coordination fees
  • Build their brand within the PorchDecor platform
  • Receive priority support and resources
  • Have input on regional product offerings

Requirements for Store Entrepreneurs

  • Established farm with 2+ years of seasonal growing experience
  • Capacity to coordinate logistics and quality control
  • Strong communication and relationship-building skills
  • Commitment to representing the PorchDecor brand
  • Ability to work with multiple growers in your region

Interested in becoming a Store Entrepreneur? This opportunity is ideal for established growers looking to expand their impact and income while supporting other local farmers.

Financial Information

Pricing Structure

You set wholesale prices for your products. We work together to ensure:

  • You receive fair compensation that covers costs plus profit
  • Prices are competitive within your region
  • Quality products command premium pricing
  • Volume commitments offer pricing stability

Payment Terms

  • Net 7 days from delivery confirmation
  • Direct deposit to your farm account
  • Transparent reporting on all transactions
  • No hidden fees or surprise deductions

Platform Fees

PorchDecor charges a platform fee that covers:

  • Marketing to thousands of potential customers
  • Technology platform and ordering system
  • Logistics coordination and support
  • Customer service and satisfaction guarantees
  • Quality control and brand standards

Platform fee: 20-25% of retail price (depending on volume and product category). You still earn significantly more than traditional wholesale channels.
